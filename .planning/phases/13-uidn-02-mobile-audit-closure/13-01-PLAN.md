Fix the `audit-screenshots.mjs` harness to eliminate the Phase 9 Plan 02 hydration-wait defect, then run it to produce a clean 42-PNG screenshot corpus.

Output: Modified `audit-screenshots.mjs`; clean 42-PNG corpus in `.planning/closure/artifacts/screenshots/`; updated `MANIFEST.json`.
